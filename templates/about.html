About Truth Behind the Tagline

Our Mission

Truth Behind the Tagline (TBT) is an AI-powered system designed to analyze marketing claims, taglines, and slogans from Indian brands to uncover the real story behind them. By combining cutting-edge NLP technology with domain research, TBT delivers transparent, data-driven insights so consumers can make informed decisions.

Our mission is to promote transparency in advertising and empower Indian consumers with factual information about the products they use daily. We believe in holding companies accountable for their marketing claims through objective analysis and evidence-based verification.

How It Works

Claim Ingestion

We analyze the brand name, tagline, and specific marketing claim.

Evidence Retrieval

Our system searches for relevant evidence from trusted sources.

AI Analysis

Advanced NLP models evaluate claims against evidence.

Verdict

We provide a clear assessment with supporting evidence.

Technology Behind TBT

Our system leverages state-of-the-art Natural Language Processing (NLP) technology and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to analyze marketing claims:

  • HuggingFace Transformers: We use advanced language models optimized for Indian English and multilingual content.
  • RAG System: Combines neural search with Indian market-specific knowledge base.
  • Indian Regulatory Framework: Our analysis considers ASCI guidelines and other Indian regulatory standards.
  • Indic NLP Support: Special processing for Indian languages and mixed-language content.
